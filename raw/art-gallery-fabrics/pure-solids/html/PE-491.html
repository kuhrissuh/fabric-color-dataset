Pure Color, Pure Softness, Pure Beauty… Our Pure Elements are now PURE SOLIDS!! The purest hues meet Art Gallery Fabrics ®’ soft hand and superior quality. Now all the solid fabric you have been looking for to match your collections are here. Ranging in a vibrant assortment of colors, our PURE SOLIDS have new characteristics you’ll love while maintaining the virtues that made them famous. Our unique and improved dip-dye technique leads to the most brilliant colors ever. Now all our PURE SOLIDS are internationally OEKO-TEX certified, so you can rest assure our dyes are the most environmentally safe to use for the sewing industry. Commonly known as “pima” our premium cottons have the softest hand and the lowest shrinkage percentage (1% – 2%). They almost don’t fray, making them ideal for all kind of projects. Our goal is always to offer you fabrics that will make you “FEEL THE DIFFERENCE”.
